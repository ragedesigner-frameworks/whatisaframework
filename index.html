What Is A Framework?

A framework is a reusable system for solving recurring problems. Instead of starting from scratch each time, frameworks give you a structured approach that produces consistent, repeatable results.

The Simple Definition

A framework is a structured approach to solving a specific type of problem, designed to be reused across similar situations.

Think of a framework like a recipe. A recipe doesn't just tell you what ingredients to useâ€”it tells you the order, the proportions, the timing. You can make the dish again and again with consistent results, and you can adapt it once you understand the underlying principles.

Business frameworks work the same way. They capture the essential steps, considerations, and decision points that lead to successful outcomes. Once you have a framework, you don't have to reinvent your approach every time you face a similar challenge.
